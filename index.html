I am a Jack Jamie of many trades.


I earned my bachelor's degree at IUPUI General Studies, with minors in psychology, sociology, and economics. In other words, I thoroughly enjoyed my time in school and spent way too much time there; I wanted to learn everything, and I couldn't make up my mind.

In college, I fell in love with social and positive psychology. I frequently re-read textbook chapters and take classes when I can free up the time. I hope to combine the creativity of coding with the psychology behind UX/UI design in a future career.

My work experience includes finance, administration, floral design, nonprofit management, grant-writing, retail, cakes, sales, bouncy houses... There isn't much I'm not willing to learn!
